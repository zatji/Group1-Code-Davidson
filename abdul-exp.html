My Code Plateau experience

On the 5th of August 2019, Code Plateau training kicked off with the venue of software development programme being at nHub Nigeria. We then immediately got grouped into 3 different groups for ease of training which is to be conducted by the instructors.


The training kicked off on a good basis, with the instructors taking their time to put us through the basics of software development and ever since, the intensity of the training has been increasing on a daily basis. I don't find this increasing intensity to b frustrating or discouraging rather I find it to be challenging for nothing good comes easy in this life.
